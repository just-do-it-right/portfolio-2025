## Overview

At SolarWinds, I served as Senior Graphic Designer, transforming complex tech concepts into compelling visual stories that resonated with our audience. I played a key role in shaping our marketing campaigns, trade show presence, and digital brand identity. From designing immersive event spaces to orchestrating full-scale campaign ecosystems, I helped establish SolarWinds as a distinctive presence in the tech landscape.

## Key Responsibilities

- **Event Design**: Elevated our trade show presence through thoughtful booth design and branding. Developed comprehensive designs for venues ranging from 10x10 pop-ups to sophisticated 40x40 custom builds at major events like AWS re:Invent and Microsoft Ignite. Created cohesive experiences from booth graphics to memorable promotional materials.
- **Digital Campaign Development**: Delivered high-performing marketing campaigns across multiple channels. Utilized Creatopy for efficient digital banner production while maintaining design excellence. Created conversion-focused landing pages that balanced visual appeal with performance.
- **Brand System Management**: Enhanced our asset management workflow using BrandFolder and Aprimo, streamlining access to brand materials for global teams. Implemented systems to maintain brand consistency across all channels and regions.
- **Team Collaboration**: Worked closely with marketing teams to transform concepts into polished deliverables, maintaining brand integrity while meeting project timelines. Effectively bridged creative vision with practical execution.

## Technical Toolkit

- **Design Software**: Advanced proficiency in Adobe Creative Suite (Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign) 
- **Digital Platforms**: Creatopy for campaign creation, BrandFolder & Aprimo for brand asset management
- **Project Management**: Asana for managing complex project timelines and cross-team collaboration
- **Event Design**: Specialized in large-format production, booth skinning, and environmental graphics
- **Web Development**: Working knowledge of HTML/CSS/Markdown for seamless collaboration with development teams

## Achievements

Significantly enhanced our event presence through strategic booth design and branding. Consistently delivered campaigns that exceeded engagement benchmarks, while our booth designs helped the events team meet and exceed their lead generation targets. Implemented efficient workflows that improved team productivity and creative output.

## Results

The implementation of streamlined design processes and robust asset management systems markedly improved our creative workflow efficiency. Through systematic improvements and strategic design thinking, we reduced production time while increasing creative output quality. Our enhanced event presence and booth designs contributed significantly to SolarWinds' brand recognition in the technology sector.
