## Overview

At ShipStation, I specialized in creating tangible brand experiences through custom apparel design and direct mail campaigns. This unique focus allowed me to bridge digital and physical brand touchpoints, creating memorable marketing materials that customers could literally wear and hold.

## Key Responsibilities

- **Apparel Design**: Conceptualized and executed creative t-shirt designs for company events, employee recognition, and customer appreciation programs, balancing brand guidelines with contemporary fashion trends.
- **Direct Mail Campaign**: Designed eye-catching printed mailers that stood out in physical mailboxes, incorporating innovative formats and finishing techniques to increase engagement.
- **Production Management**: Coordinated with print vendors and apparel manufacturers to ensure high-quality production while managing costs and timelines.
- **Brand Consistency**: Maintained brand integrity across various physical mediums while pushing creative boundaries in merchandise design.

## Technical Toolkit

- **Design Software**: Adobe Creative Suite (Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign) for print and apparel design
- **Print Production**: Expert knowledge of print specifications, paper stocks, and specialty finishing techniques
- **Apparel Design**: Understanding of screen printing, embroidery, and other textile decoration methods
- **Color Management**: Mastery of both CMYK print production and specialty ink systems for apparel

## Achievements

Successfully developed a series of highly sought-after t-shirt designs that became collector's items among employees and customers. Created direct mail campaigns that achieved above-average response rates through innovative design and formatting. Established efficient workflows for managing physical product production.

## Results

The apparel program became a cornerstone of company culture and customer appreciation efforts, while the direct mail campaigns provided a tangible complement to digital marketing initiatives. These physical brand touchpoints significantly enhanced brand recognition and customer engagement.
