## Overview

As Graphic Design and Production Specialist at Hellas Construction, I developed innovative marketing materials for the nation's leading sports facility construction company. Working across multiple brands including Matrix Turf and epiQ Tracks, I created compelling visual narratives that showcased our expertise in artificial turf and synthetic surfaces while documenting high-profile NFL and sports facility installations.

## Key Responsibilities

- **Marketing Materials**: Created comprehensive marketing pieces including brochures, emails, landing pages, whitepapers, product guides, flyers, and large-scale signage for direct marketing and printed support tools.
- **Brand Development**: Managed distinct visual identities for Matrix Turf, epiQ Tracks, and other product lines while maintaining consistent corporate branding across all channels.
- **Sports Facility Documentation**: Captured and art directed photography of major installations, with heavy emphasis on sports-related material and NFL facility projects.
- **Web Content**: Designed, updated, and maintained website graphics and content across multiple brand websites (hellasconstruction.com, matrix-turf.com, epiqtracks.com), ensuring consistent brand presentation.

## Technical Toolkit

- **Design Software**: Advanced proficiency in Adobe Creative Suite (InDesign, Photoshop, Illustrator, Acrobat) on Mac Platform
- **Digital Design**: Web graphics, email templates, and landing page design with SEO optimization
- **Photography**: Sports facility and construction documentation
- **Project Management**: Expertise in handling multiple projects under tight deadlines

## Achievements

Successfully managed the visual presentation of multiple brand identities while maintaining consistent corporate standards. Created compelling marketing materials that effectively communicated technical product features and installation processes. Established comprehensive documentation practices for high-profile sports facility projects.

## Results

Developed and implemented design systems that improved marketing effectiveness across all brands and channels. Created a robust library of sports facility photography and marketing materials that strengthened Hellas's position as a leader in athletic facility construction. Streamlined production processes for faster turnaround on marketing materials while maintaining high quality standards.
