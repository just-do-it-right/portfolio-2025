## Overview

At Cloak, I championed privacy-first design principles while creating compelling visual narratives around digital security. This unique position allowed me to bridge the gap between complex security concepts and user-friendly design, making privacy advocacy accessible and engaging for a broader audience.

## Key Responsibilities

- **Privacy-Focused Design**: Developed visual systems that communicated security concepts clearly while maintaining user trust and confidentiality principles.
- **Educational Materials**: Created infographics and visual guides that transformed complex privacy topics into easily digestible content for users of all technical levels.
- **Brand Identity**: Crafted a visual identity that balanced security with approachability, helping to demystify privacy technology for everyday users.
- **User Experience**: Designed interfaces and materials that promoted privacy best practices while maintaining an intuitive and friendly user experience.

## Technical Toolkit

- **Design Software**: Adobe Creative Suite (Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign) for security-focused design
- **Information Design**: Creation of clear, educational visuals explaining complex privacy concepts
- **UI/UX Design**: Development of user interfaces that prioritized both security and usability
- **Iconography**: Custom icon design for representing privacy and security features

## Achievements

Successfully translated complex privacy and security concepts into accessible visual communications that resonated with users. Created educational materials that increased user engagement with privacy features while maintaining technical accuracy. Developed a brand identity that made privacy technology more approachable without compromising its serious nature.

## Results

The design work contributed to increased user adoption of privacy features and better understanding of security concepts. The visual system and educational materials became key tools in advocating for digital privacy, helping users make informed decisions about their online security.
